Puff-Puff Recipe

A popular Nigerian deep-fried snack, sweet and fluffy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, nutmeg, and salt to the yeast mixture and mix until smooth.
  3. Cover the batter and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan and scoop batter into the oil using a spoon.
  5. Fry until golden brown, turning occasionally.
  6. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
