Egusi Soup Recipe

A delicious and hearty Nigerian soup made with ground melon seeds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 500g assorted meat (beef, goat, tripe)
  • 1 smoked fish, deboned
  • 1 cup palm oil
  • 2 cups spinach or bitter leaf
  • 2 tablespoons crayfish, ground
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers, blended
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • Salt and seasoning cubes to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat palm oil in a pot and saut√© onions and garlic.
  2. Add ground egusi and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in the beef stock and stir well.
  4. Add the cooked meat, smoked fish, crayfish, and blended peppers.
  5. Simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in the spinach or bitter leaf and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot with pounded yam or fufu.
