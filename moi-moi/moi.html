Moi Moi Recipe

A nutritious and delicious Nigerian steamed bean pudding.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups peeled black-eyed peas
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs (optional)
  • 1 cup fish or meat (optional)
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground crayfish
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. Blend the beans, bell pepper, and onion with water until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and add oil, salt, seasoning cubes, and crayfish.
  3. Mix well and add optional eggs or meat.
  4. Pour into moi moi containers or banana leaves.
  5. Steam for about 45 minutes until firm.
  6. Allow to cool slightly before serving.
